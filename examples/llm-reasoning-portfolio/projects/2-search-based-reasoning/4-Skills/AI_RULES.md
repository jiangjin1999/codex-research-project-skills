# Project AI Rules — 2-search-based-reasoning

## Scope

Inference-time search over reasoning steps (Tree/Graph of Thoughts) on small public tasks.

## Do

- Stay in literature-framing until the ToT vs GoT trade-off note is complete.
- Classify before writing: attempt → `0-Project/`, method → `1-Docs/`, paper → `3-Paper_Survey/`.
- Cap the search/step budget in any first attempt; record cost alongside accuracy.

## Don't

- Don't launch large search runs before a small task and budget are fixed.
- Don't put raw traces or dataset rows into shared files or the board.
